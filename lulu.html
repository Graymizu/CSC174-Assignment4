TOUR

I am a academic coach for the program called TOUR. It stands for Transitional Opportunties for students at University of Rochester.

My Role

As a academic coach I audit a course with a student in TOUR and we take a college course together. I also meet with them outside of class time for a hour or so every week to help them keep up in the class. As a coach, my primary job is to help my mentee reach the goal that they want for the particular class they are taking.
